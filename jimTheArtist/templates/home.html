Jim Militello, Landscape Artist


About Me

Jim's paintings are done on location here in our region, whether it be local woodlands, seaside vistas, or trips to Appalachian Mountains. He aspires to capture a place or moment in time. He finds compelling subject matter not in distant or exotic travels, but in the natural world all around us seen on almost a daily basis, but often unnoticed. The work is therapeutic; a respite from the pressures and complexities of every day living. This is true for the artist in experiencing the creative process…he hopes also true for those who view his work.
